It's not just a storybook
It's stARybook

The stARybook is an interactive application that was specially developed for children's storybook. This application has Augmented Reality and other multimedia elements for children to explore while reading storybooks. The purpose of this application is to stimulate young children's interest in reading books and to expose them to multimedia in a young age. The user manual, e-book, and applications for the kids should be downloaded and installed by the parents. Scan the QR Code now!
